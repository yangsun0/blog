LEGO Tree House

December 01, 2019

This is one of my favorite LEGO set: Tree House. Look at the pictures, the tree house has so many details. One special thing about this lego set is the idea is not from the lego company, the idea is from lego fans. The LEGO fans made the tree house, and submit his idea to LEGO company. Once he got 10K supporters from the other LEGO fans, the LEGO company made this as real LEGO product.
